Let’s Begin

Published Jun 13, 2020 by Dave Genu at https://functionfollows.com/001-lets-begin/

Today, I’ve chosen to publish something online for the first time in a long time.

I don’t necessarily know what that will be, yet. I haven’t been struck with inspiration or taken by a fell mood. Consistency will not be an aspect of this website (both in terms of entry usefulness and entry timeliness). Conciseness will not be an aspect of this website. These are just the idle musings of an American man. My words will have no greater meaning. My ideas will have no greater impact.

Consider the following entries to be a type of “conscious slag”. Through a standard workday, I design and build multi-axis machines for use all over the world. This activity takes my major focus because it is the one thing separating me from being financially destitute and mentally unmotivated. The valuable resource smelted in the foundry of my skull is this ability to design and implement engineered solutions for cutting-edge companies. At the end of the day, once this resource has been successfully tapped out through a metaphorical trepan (the process typically takes 8 earth hours), I am left with a cavernous crucible of conscious slag.

It’s not garbage. It’s just not being put to good use. Is there a good use for this slag? I’m not very certain.

Regardless, that’s the idea. Take the conscious slag, scrape it from the bottom of the crucible, throw it into a unique refinery machine (design TBD), and post the result online. Maybe it’ll be useful. Maybe it won’t. The only way to know is to do it and find out. I’ve recently been struck by the sense of just hitting publish. Perhaps this article had some impact: It’s Never Done, Thus It’s Already Done (The layout of that blog may look similar, the author is the creator of this layout).

There will be some growing pains in the meantime. Pardon the dust.
